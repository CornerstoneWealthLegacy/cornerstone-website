Accessibility Statement

Last Updated: May 2026

Cornerstone Wealth & Legacy Law, PLLC is committed to ensuring that our website is accessible to people of all abilities, including those who use assistive technologies such as screen readers and keyboard navigation. We believe everyone should be able to access information about planning for their family and their future.

Our Commitment

We strive to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA, published by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C). These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with disabilities and more user-friendly for everyone.

Measures We Take

  • Using semantic HTML and descriptive headings to support screen-reader navigation.
  • Providing text alternatives for meaningful images.
  • Maintaining sufficient color contrast for text and interface elements.
  • Supporting keyboard navigation for menus and interactive controls.
  • Using clear, plain language wherever possible.

Ongoing Effort

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. We periodically review the site and work to address areas that can be improved. As we add new features and content, we aim to maintain and improve accessibility across the site.

Third-Party Content

Some portions of the site rely on third-party tools and services, such as scheduling, payment processing, and document features. We do not control the accessibility of third-party platforms, but we are happy to assist you in accessing information or completing any task by an alternative method.

Need Assistance?

If you encounter any difficulty using this website, or if you need information or services in an alternative format, please contact us. We will work with you to provide the information, document, or service you need through a method that works for you.
