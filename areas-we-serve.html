Areas We Serve

Cornerstone Wealth & Legacy Law serves estate planning, elder law, and probate clients throughout Florida — handled conveniently by phone, video, or appointment. Our roots are in the Daytona Beach area, and we work closely with families across Volusia and Flagler counties.

Free 20-Minute Consultation Call (386) 293-5586

Florida estate plans are governed by the same statewide law no matter where you live, so we are able to prepare wills, trusts, powers of attorney, health care directives, and probate filings for clients anywhere in the state. Below are the communities we work with most often. Each page covers the local courthouse, the county where probate is filed, and the issues that matter most to residents there.
